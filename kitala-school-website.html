About Our School

Building Character & Academic Excellence

Sure Prospective Secondary School Kitala is a premier educational institution located along the Entebbe Road in Uganda. We are committed to providing a holistic, high-quality education that prepares students for Uganda's national examinations and life beyond.

Our school blends rigorous academics with values education, sport, arts, and community service — equipping every student with the skills, confidence, and moral grounding to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

🎯 Our MissionTo provide quality education that develops all-round individuals
🌟 Our VisionA centre of academic and moral excellence in East Africa
📖 Core ValuesIntegrity · Excellence · Respect · Service
🇺🇬 AccreditationLicensed by UNEB & Ministry of Education Uganda

Admissions 2026

Join Our School Family

We welcome bright and motivated students to be part of our growing community of learners. Applications for S.1, S.4 and S.5 entry for the 2026 academic year are now open.

1
Download Form
Get the application form from our school office or download it from our website
2
Submit Documents
Bring completed form with academic reports, birth certificate & passport photos
3
Entrance Assessment
Sit for a short placement assessment and interview with our admissions team
4
Receive Offer Letter
Successful applicants receive an offer letter within 5 working days to confirm enrolment

📋 Requirements Checklist

✅
Completed application form (from office or download)
✅
Certified copies of most recent academic reports (PLE/UCE)
✅
Original birth certificate or valid national ID
✅
Two recent passport-size photographs
✅
Letter of recommendation from previous school
✅
Non-refundable application fee of UGX 30,000
